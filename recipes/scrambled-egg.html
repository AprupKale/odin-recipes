Scrambled Egg

Description

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from the whisked together yolks and whites of eggs, sometimes combined with butter, cream, or cheese, and cooked over low or medium heat (never high heat!) to achieve varying textures.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Break the eggs in a bowl
  2. Add milk and salt
  3. Microwave the bowl, remove the bowl and beat the eggs - repeat until desired consistency
